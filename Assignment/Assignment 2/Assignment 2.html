Gojo Satoru

Gojo Satoru is a teacher at Tokyo Jujutsu High in Jujutsu Kaisen, known for his playful and confident personality while deeply caring for his students. He possesses near-godlike powers, including the Limitless/Infinity technique and the Six Eyes, which make him almost untouchable. Using abilities like Red, Blue, and Hollow Purple, he can effortlessly defeat enemies. Considered the strongest Jujutsu sorcerer, Gojo perfectly balances humor with overwhelming strength, making him both a mentor and a powerhouse in the series.
